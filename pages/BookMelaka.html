Visiting Zoo Melaka in Malaysia

Head to the second-largest zoo in Malaysia! Located around 1.5 hours away from the capital city Kuala Lumpur, close to Malacca City is Zoo Melaka. Operational since 1963 under the government, the zoo was privatized in 2013 and new operating procedures were put in place. A great venue for a family or educational outing, read on to learn more about Zoo Melaka tickets, timings, what to see, what to do and how to get there

Why Visit Zoo Melaka?

Gamuda Cove is open daily, but the attractions within it have varying timings:

  • Largest Zoo: Spanning 22 hectares, the Melaka Zoo is the second-largest zoo in the country.
  • Open Concept: The zoo follows an open concept, meaning the landscape mirrors tropical vegetation, and you can watch the animals in their natural rainforest habitat as opposed to cages
  • Night Safari: The zoo has included a night safari in its list of offerings, for visitors to observe the habits of nocturnal animals as well.
  • Educational Tours: Melaka Zoo regularly hosts educational tours for visitors and students to get familiar with wildlife.
  • Interactive Activities: Take part in various interactive activities such as elephant feeding, bird shows and zookeeper talks




All Your Zoo Melaka Tickets Explained


Admission Tickets to Zoo Melaka

  • Discover the wonders of nature at Zoo Melaka, an open-concept zoo where guests can interact with the friendly residents at this Malaysia’s oldest zoo
  • With your Zoo Melaka tickets, you can get entry to the unique sanctuary of wildlife, as you wander the 54-acre area, which is home to over 1,200 animals from over 200 species.
  • The tickets get you access to the Day Zoo, the Dinosaur Encounter, and the Animal Attractive. You can add the Melaka Zoo Night Safari at an additional cost to explore the zoo’s nocturnal species and nightlife.
  • Learn interesting facts about prehistoric creatures at the Dinosaur Encounter Zone and watch in awe the realistically moving life-size dinosaurs.

Combo: Zoo Melaka + Melaka Crocodile & ​​Recreational Park Entry Tickets

  • Get your hands on this pocket-friendly combo which allows you to get up and close with nature’s residents at Zoo Melaka and Melaka Crocodile and Recreational Park
  • With your Combo Zoo and Park entry tickets, you can enjoy a wildlife adventure at Zoo Melaka, where you can find animals in their almost natural habitat, and Melaka Crocodile and Recreational Park, which allows you to learn about crocodiles from 4 different species.
  • At Zoo Melaka, watch and interact with animals from over 200 species, ranging from deer, rabbits, emus, and more at various dedicated zones

Address: Sunway Lagoon, Sunway Pyramid, 3, Bandar Sunway, Jalan PJS 11/11, 46150 Petaling Jaya, Selangor, Malaysia.

  • By Car: With fast expressways, Sunway Lagoon is a short 15-minute drive from Kuala Lumpur. You can simply put in the location of the park and the navigation will take you to your destination. 
  • By Train: Take the KTM Komuter train to Subang Jaya Station or the Putra LRT train to Kelana Jaya station, then the Sunway Pyramid feeder bus to the park. Alternatively, you can take a train from Kelana Jaya Station and board a feeder bus to the Pyramid stop.
  • By Bus: Sunway Pyramid stop is the closest bus stop to the park and is only a 4-minute walk away.
  • By Shuttle Service: Free shuttle service is provided by Sunway around the city every 25 minutes. It operates between 7 AM and 9:30 PM from Monday to Friday, and between 9 AM and 9:30 PM on weekends and public holidays.
  • WiFi: There is free WiFi available at Sunway Lagoon
  • Wheelchair accessibility: The park is mostly wheelchair accessible. They also have accessible toilets.
  • Parking: Parking is available at Sunway Pyramid Shopping Mall & Sunway Resort Hotel & Spa.
  • Lockers: You can store your belongings in lockers while visiting the park. You can get a locker for specific time slots. 
  • Washrooms: There are washrooms where you can change after the water pool ride.
  • First Aid: There is a private hospital located near the park for any medical emergencies. 
  • Park Map: There is a map of the park available to make sure that you don’t get lost in the park. Download the map here.
