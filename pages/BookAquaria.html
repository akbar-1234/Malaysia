Explore Aquaria KLCC: Kuala Lumpur's aquatic wonderland

Aquaria KLCC, situated beneath the iconic Petronas Twin Towers in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, is a marine sanctuary that beckons travelers and marine enthusiasts alike. With over 5,000 aquatic creatures from across the globe, it's a hub of underwater biodiversity. The highlight is a 90-meter-long transparent tunnel, offering a walk surrounded by sharks, rays, and other marine marvels. On this page, you'll discover a range of ticket options, essential information about Aquaria KLCC, its top attractions, and helpful tips to ensure your visit is both enjoyable and educational. Dive in with Aquaria KLCC Tickets!

Why visit Aquaria KLCC?

Underwater wonderland: Immerse yourself in an underwater world at Aquaria KLCC, where you'll encounter diverse marine species from seahorses to massive sharks

Tunnel adventure: Walk through a 90-meter-long transparent tunnel surrounded by oceanic giants, offering a breathtaking view of aquatic life swimming above and around you

Educational experience: Learn about marine conservation and the importance of protecting our oceans through interactive exhibits and informative displays

Touch pools: Get up-close and personal with aquatic creatures like starfish and sea cucumbers at the touch pools, a favorite for kids and curious adults alike

Thrilling dive shows: Witness dive performances by skilled aquarists, showcasing feeding sessions with ferocious predators, providing an up-close encounter you won't forget.

Top Aquaria KLCC attractions

Aquaria KLCC offers a captivating journey through aquatic wonders. Encounter diverse marine life, stroll through a tunnel surrounded by sharks, and explore interactive exhibits that educate and inspire. This overview promises an aquatic adventure for visitors of all ages. If you want to learn more about these attractions, visit this page.

Evolution Zone

In this zone, you’ll be seeing red-bellied piranhas. Learn all about their history, why they aren’t actually that scary and their flesh-eating tendencies.

The Stream

Be enchanted by a tranquil aquatic stream, where graceful creatures glide through crystal-clear waters, creating a serene and picturesque scene.

Touch Zone

Get up close and personal with fascinating marine life, from gentle starfish to intriguing sea cucumbers, offering an interactive and memorable experience for all.

Jewels of Jungle

Step into a lush, aquatic rainforest, where vibrant and exotic underwater flora and fauna create a dazzling display of nature's beauty.

Shipwreck

Dive into the depths of a sunken ship, where you'll uncover hidden treasures and eerie mysteries of the deep sea in a thrilling underwater adventure.

The Coastal

Explore the meeting point of land and sea, where mangrove forests and vibrant coral reefs converge, teeming with life and vibrant colors.

Rules and regulations
  • Aquaria KLCC’s opening hours are from 10 AM to 8 PM
  • Last entry to the aquarium is at 7 PM
  • Aquaria KLCC does not close during public holidays

Best time to visit

The park is crowded from October to January. Avoid visiting on weekends and public holidays. Visit the park on the weekdays during the off-season (December - April) to have a pleasant time and enjoy all the attractions.

  • Aquaria KLCC Location: Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre, Jalan Pinang, Kuala Lumpur City Centre, 50088 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia 
  • Find on map
  • LRT Train: Accessible via Kelana Jaya Line LRT, disembark at KLCC LRT Station (KJ10), a 7-10 minute walk to Aquaria KLCC.
  • MRT: Nearest MRT station is Bukit Bintang Station (SBK 18A), a 10-minute journey to Aquaria KLCC.
  • Monorail: Raja Chulan station is the closest monorail stop. Follow directions for a 7-10 minute walk to Aquaria KLCC.
  • Bus: Take the Green Line Bus to Kuala Lumpur Convention Center bus stop, then walk to Aquaria KLCC. Alternatively, use the Kuala Lumpur Hop on Hop off Tour Bus, alight near the West Wing of Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre at KLCC Garden Park stop.
  • Car/Taxi: Arrive at the drop-off area near Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre. An escalator to the concourse level leads to the main entrance of Aquaria KLCC.
  • Wheelchair Access: Aquaria KLCC is wheelchair-friendly, with ramps and elevators for accessibility.
  • Prayer Room: A designated prayer room is available within the facility.
  • Restrooms: Well-maintained restrooms are conveniently located for visitors.
  • Information Desks: Information counters are positioned to assist visitors with inquiries and guidance.
  • Souvenir Shop: Explore a souvenir shop offering aquatic-themed mementos.
  • Food Court: Enjoy dining options within the premises for a quick meal.
  • Educational Programs: Aquaria KLCC provides educational programs for learning opportunities.
