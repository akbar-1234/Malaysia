Ride the Awana SkyWay Gondola With Your Genting Highlands Cable Car Tickets

The Genting Cable Car is an aerial gondola lift system that takes visitors up to the Genting Highlands. It is one of two cable car routes that travel this route (the Genting SkyWay is the other). Connecting Awana SkyCentral and SkyAvenue near the mountain peak, it covers a journey of 2.8 kilometers, traveling over a scenic rainforest

  • There are truly few places in the world you'll get to see a 130 million year old rainforest from 620 metres high in the sky - an experience you get to have with the Genting Cable Car and the Awana SkyWay!
  • The Genting SkyWay is one of the longest cable car ropeways in South-East Asia
  • Aboard the Genting Cable Car and the Awana SkyWay, you can fly up from Awana Station to the SkyAvenue Station in the Genting Highlands, which takes around 10 minutes
  • From the gondola lift, you can see the picturesque hilly landscape unfold below you and enjoy the crisp mountain air of the Genting Highlands
  • With your Genting Cable Car ticket, you can also stop at the Chin Swee Station, where you can explore the beautiful Chin Swee Caves Temple at no additional cost

Your Genting Cable Car Tickets Explained

Genting Awana SkyWay Tickets

  • Get admission to the Awana SkyWay cable car and enjoy breathtaking views of the Genting Highlands and a 130-million-year-old rainforest
  • You will also get complimentary access to the Chin Swee Caves Temple, a Taoist temple spread across 28-acres of the mountainside
  • Make use of the free charging stations on the gondolas in case your mobile phone dies
  • Upgrade to ride in a glass-bottom gondola and get unique views of the scenic landscapes below

Genting Awana SkyWay Combo Tickets

  • With a single ticket, you’ll get to experience multiple must-try experiences in Kuala Lumpur
  • Travel on the scenic 10-minute Awana SkyWay cable car ride over the Genting Highlands
  • You can also stop off along the way at the Chin Swee Caves Temple, at no extra cost
  • Depending on your ticket of choice, you get to experience more iconic Kuala Lumpur attractions at once along with your Genting Awana SkyWay experience!

Genting Highlands Day Trip

  • Experience the thrilling Genting Cable Car to observe Genting Highlands from an amazing bird's eye view to start off your day trip
  • Explore the many themeparks located at Genting Highlands, providing oodles of fun for the whole family!
  • Head to the casino resort, Casino de Genting for another exciting round of fun during your day trip

What to Expect on Your Genting Cable Car Experience

  • Prepare to embark on your aerial journey to the exciting Genting Highlands from your cable car route (Genting SkyWay or Awana SkyWay)
  • You will be taken across 2-3 kilometres on your cable car ride in the span of 10-15 minutes (again, depending on your cable car route)
  • You can marvel upon some stunning views of the rainforest below as you are lifted gently in the air (over 620 metres!).
  • You can also disembark at the mid-way point to explore the Chin Swee Caves Temple, a popular tourist attraction in the area.

Operating Hours: 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM*


Duration: A cable car ride takes about 10 minutes.

*Chin Swee Station operates from 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM

The Genting Highlands has the mildest weather between the months of March and September

To get the best views of the surrounding mountains, try to go on the cable car ride in the early morning when the weather is clear

Address: Level 4, Awana SkyCentral, 69000 Genting Highlands, Bentong, Pahang, Malaysia | Find On Map

The Genting Highlands Cable Car starts from Awana Station, which is situated on Level 4 of the Awana Transport Hub (also called Awana SkyCentral) in Pahang. It will take you about 1 hour to reach Awana Station from KL Sentral

By Bus:

  • To take the Genting SkyWay, you have to take a bus from KL Sentral to Gohtong Jaya Genting SkyWay Complex
  • To take the Awana SkyWay, take a bus from KL Sentral to Awana Bus Terminal

By taxi:

  • For the Genting SkyWay, a taxi ride from Kuala Lumpur would be around 41 minutes long
  • For the Awana SkyWay, a taxi ride from Kuala Lumpur would be around 44 minutes long
  • Children who are below 90 cm tall can ride for free.
  • Smoking of any sort is strictly prohibited throughout the journey
  • Guests under 16 years of age must be accompanied by an adult with valid tickets at all times
  • Pets are prohibited at all stations and inside the gondola.
  • When stopping off at Chin Swee Station, please keep your ticket handy in order to continue your journey
  • Each passenger is allowed to bring along one aircraft cabin size bag into the gondola. Large bags will be subject to a surcharge of RM20

Lockers: Any bags and luggage can be stored at the Baggage Storage Service at the Awana SkyCentral building for RM5 per bag for 24 hours. Lockers are also available on Level 2 of SkyAvenue

Toilets: There are a number of restrooms available at Awana SkyCentral, including at Awana Station on level 4

Baby Changing Station: Most of the restrooms have changing facilities for children. At SkyCentral they are available on Levels 1, 3, and 4.

Charging Stations: There are free charging ports available at the stations and on the gondolas themselves

  • Awana SkyCental and SkyAvenue Station are both wheelchair accessible
  • You can also rent a wheelchair from Level 1 of SkyAvenue. A deposit of RM100 is required, but this will be refunded upon the return of the wheelchair
  • However, of the two types of Awana SkyWay gondolas, only the standard ones are large enough to accommodate wheelchair users while the glass-floor gondolas are not

Legoland Malaysia: If you’re still in the mood for theme parks, Legoland Malaysia is another great place to just let loose and have fun. With more than eight themed sections with over 40 activities and attractions, Legoland Malaysia is definitely a must-visit! 

Habitat Penang Hill: Deep dive into nature’s lap at the Habitat Penang Hill, a magnificent rainforest discovery center, which serves as a perfect place for all nature enthusiasts.

Entopia by Penang Butterfly Farm: Get closer to nature with a classroom made of nature at Entopia. With more than 300 species of flora and fauna, indulge in learning activities and interact with animals to learn more about wonderful nature.
