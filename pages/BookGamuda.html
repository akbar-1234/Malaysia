Discover The Enchanting Gamuda Cove

Gamuda Cove is a 1,500-acre township located in Kuala Langat, Malaysia. It is a nature sanctuary and smart city that is home to a variety of attractions, including a water park, a discovery park, and a rich wetland. The water park, called SplashMania, is a popular tourist destination that offers a variety of thrilling water slides and attractions. Discovery Park is a great place to learn about the local flora and fauna, and the wetlands, named Payah Indah Discovery Wetlands are home to diverse wildlife and many educational activities that will leave you mesmerized. On this page, you will find information about Gamuda Cove, including ticket options, information about attractions, and tips for making your visit a success.

Top Things to do at Gamuda Cove

SplashMania

This water park is home to a variety of rides and slides, including the world's tallest water coaster, the Tsunami Racer.

The Cove

This man-made lake is perfect for swimming, kayaking, and paddleboarding. There's also a sandy beach area where you can relax and soak up the sun.

The Boardwalk

This waterfront promenade is lined with shops, restaurants, and cafes. There's also a playground for kids and a splash pad for toddlers.

The Forest

This lush green space is home to a variety of plants and animals. There are hiking trails, a playground, and a picnic area.

The Gamuda Cove Farmers Market

This market is held every weekend and features a variety of local produce, food, and crafts.

The Gamuda Cove Night Market

This market is held every Friday and Saturday night and features a variety of street food, drinks, and entertainment.

Visitor Tips & Guidelines

Gamuda Cove is open daily, but the attractions within it have varying timings:

  • SplashMania: SplashMania Water Park is open from 10 AM to 6 PM. Last entry for the park is at 5:30 PM.
  • Discovery Park: Monday to Sunday: 10 AM - 6 PM. Last entry to the park is at 5 PM. The park is closed on Tuesdays for maintenance.
  • Payah Indah Discovery Wetlands: The wetlands are open daily from 9 AM to 5 PM.

What is the best time to visit Gamuda Cove?

If you're flexible with your travel dates, consider visiting Gamuda Cove during the off-season. (November - March) The park will be less crowded and you'll likely get a better deal on tickets.
